JOLLOF RICE

Discription

Jollof rice is a type of rice that is usually red in colour and eaten with a little bit of shito. it is tasty.

It is usually eaten in the southern part of the country, however most northerners also eat it, adding their own special ingredient called the dawadawa which makes the food very tasty.

Ingredients

some of the ingredients used in the preparation of jollof are;

  • stew
  • Rice
  • salt
  • oil

Steps in preparing Jollof

  1. boil water
  2. when it boils, add rice
  3. cook stew.
